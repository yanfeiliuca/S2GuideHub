S2 Guide Hub does not require user accounts and does not ask visitors to submit personal information to use the website.

The homepage may store a language preference in your browser using localStorage. This stays on your device and helps the site remember whether you selected English or Simplified Chinese.

If you email the site, your email address and message content will be used to respond to your request, review corrections, or follow up on site issues.
