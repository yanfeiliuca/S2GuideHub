Quick call

Get Improved Fins when your Alien Ruins route is already open

Improved Fins are a strong Blueprint Atlas candidate because movement speed affects almost every later dive: oxygen margin, collection loops, creature escapes, and the time it takes to return to the Tadpole when a route gets messy.

The current source picture points to a clustered route around the Alien Ruins area, but S2GuideHub is keeping exact coordinates and fastest-stop order unpublished until the route is checked in the current Early Access build.
